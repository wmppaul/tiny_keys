Tiny Keys

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Tiny Keys is a simple iPhone piano utility. This app does not require an account and does not collect personal information from you.

Data Collection

Tiny Keys does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not:

  • require account registration
  • include analytics or advertising SDKs
  • track your activity across apps or websites
  • upload your usage, audio, or note input to a server

Audio and Device Access

Tiny Keys uses Apple's audio frameworks so it can play notes and mix with other audio already playing on your device. The app does not record microphone audio and does not store recordings.

Purchases

Tiny Keys does not include subscriptions, in-app purchases, or ads.

Children

Tiny Keys does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If Tiny Keys changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated and the revised version will be posted at this URL.
