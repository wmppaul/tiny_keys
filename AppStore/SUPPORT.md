## About The App

Tiny Keys is designed for quick melody checking by ear on iPhone. It supports:

- a minimal on-screen piano keyboard
- multitouch chords
- glissando-style note dragging
- piano, sine, triangle, and square sounds
- playback that mixes with other audio already playing on the device
